6. Shadow calculation
Use:
ShadowLength = TotalHeight / tan(SunAltitude)
Use:
TotalHeight = 250 meters.
Cap maximum shadow length at 5000 meters.
Draw long semi-transparent elliptical shadows extending away from the sun direction.
Shadows should:
- start at the turbine base
- stretch away from the sun
- fade out gradually
- be darker near the turbine
- become longer when sun altitude is lower
Add note:
"Skuggor visas som ungefärlig visualisering, inte som exakt skuggberäkning."

Add a toggle button:
📏 Verklig storlek
When ON:
- use real-world scale as much as possible
- keep turbine visual size based on actual height, distance and field of view
- show a small label:
  "Skala: verklig storlek, ungefärlig GPS/kompass"

  "Skala: förstärkt visning"

Add a toggle button:
👁️ Synlighet
When opened, show three options:
Klart väder
Dis
Dimma

- Klart väder: turbines are sharp and visible at long distance
- Dis: turbines fade slightly with distance
- Dimma: turbines fade strongly with distance

Default:
